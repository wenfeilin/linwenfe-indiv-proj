# Personas

## Persona #1: Princess Bubbles III

- 200 years old but looks and feels 23
- someone who really likes journaling (decorative) and treats it almost like a scrapbook
- also loves music and making spreads with the music as the theme
- is always on the hunt for her new favorite music
- has her whole room decorated; covers any item she owns with stickers, wash tape, glitter, etc.
- if she could, she would turn her room upside-down
- she doesn't like empty spaces
- feels a sense of pride rereading/revisiting her journal and so does this often
- never misses a day of journaling -- it's her hobby but also her livelihood; just thinking about journaling makes her so excited that she can't wait to journal at night. Yes, she journals at night although she'd prefer to journal in the morning b/c it excites her so much... BUT she wants to have enough to write about to fill up at least 3 pages for each entry, so she holds herself back until her nightly journaling session
- does not like to be interrupted while journaling and likes to be immersed in music when journaling
- enjoys eating a thick, creamy bowl of potato soup and drinking light berry drinks

## Persona #2: Andy Frankenstein

- 32 year old giant
- big in stature but small in presence
- someone who doesn't journal often but doesn't mind doing it once in a while to capture the fun moments in life
- likes to keep things minimalistic
- likes to listen to music but would struggle to associate/pick out a song for each entry -- only would choose a song for an entry if he loved that one song so much he couldn't get it out of his head for a weeks
- he lives in a little castle and surrounds himself with nature
- on most days, he finds himself staring at the bright, blue sky; he likes to daydream while staring at the big, white fluffy clouds up above. at night, he stargazes at the roof of his castle in a yellow-orange tent he has set up there just for this activity
- he is always accompanied by a little yellow bird but he doesn't know it (the bird is always quietly following him)
- doesn't like to get stuck in the weeds with the detail of anything
- if he has to decorate something with stickers, prefers to stick one big sticker onto it than multiple smaller ones
- loses track of time easily so always bring a tiny vintage pocket watch in his right pants pocket
- enjoys a hearty beef stew loaded with large chunks of boiled carrots
- appreciates life in the moment
- likes to keep journals despite all of them having numerous empty pages; takes meaning in the empty pages of his journals -- looks at them with a smile on his face
