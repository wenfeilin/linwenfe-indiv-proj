# Setup

## Observation

Use this journaling web app for the journaling parts: [Journey](https://journey.cloud)

### Task 1 (Journaling Aspect)

Write a journal entry.

### Task 2 (Music Aspect)

Find a song to represent your mood, one to represent how this past week was for you, and one that you've been enjoying these days.

### Task 3 (Combine Journaling and Music Aspects)

Write a journal entry specifically for yesterday, choose a song for this entry, and put that song in your entry. Do this in whatever order feels most natural.

### Other Tasks

Figure out how to delete an entry.

Create multiple entries.

Explore possible customizations for an entry and for the whole journal.

## Interview Questions

1. What are your journaling habits? (What do you write about, how often do you journal, etc.) If you don't journal, if you were to start today, what would your journaling style be?

2. Do you listen to music often and why? How do you pick out what songs to listen to (in general, for the day)? What makes you want to go back to a song, and how do you remember it/save it somewhere?

3. For you, what are the challenges of journaling every day? What is enjoyable about it? If you don't journal, try to imagine doing it every day.

4. For you, what are the challenges of choosing what music to listen to? What is enjoyable about it?

5. Transitioning from physical (paper) to digital journaling, what features of digital journaling do you like or would you want that'd make using it more appealing or just as appealing as physical journaling?

**Introduce app idea**: digital journaling w/ music; pick a song for each journal entry and have a calendar view of your entries where you can play the songs in your entries from, like playing a playlist of the month; can also play the current song you picked for your entry while writing it (like on loop if that is desired).

6. Does this app make you more excited about journaling and/or listening to/discovering music? Why or why not?

7. Would you find it difficult to pick out a song for every entry? (Are there reasons you could see for not wanting to use the music aspect or the journaling aspect of this app?)

8. What features do you expect when you think about a digital product made for journaling that incorporates music?

9. What types of customization would want to make this digital journal feel like yours?
