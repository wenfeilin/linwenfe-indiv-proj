# Experiment Playbook for Experiment 1

## Research Question

Is navigating through the entries via the calendar intuitive? Is there anything else the calendar UI should have to meet the user's needs?

## Script

You started this music journal on September 13th, 2025. Some of your entries so far are written in, some with a song selected, some with both, and the rest with nothing. 

From this calendar view, navigate through these four types of entries that you have. 

## Rules for Interaction

To limit the scope of this experiment, only the components that are colored can be clicked on. You will use a pencil to act as a cursor. When you want to click on something, tap on what you're clicking on. If the click was valid, change will occur on the paper prototype.

## Data To Be Collected

- time spent to complete the task
- notes on any difficulties encountered/unintended actions done
- a rating of the overall experience plus an explanation of that rating
  - what they liked
  - what they didn't like
  - did they do what they intended to do?
- answers to the research question(s)
